About BBC

Bharat Bijili Corporation (BBC) is a private distributor of power to local residents in Pune. We serve over 10,000 families in the local township.

BBC's Back Office Operations team, consisting of 20 dedicated employees, manages electricity bill payments, customer data, invoices, and payment transactions.

Our IT Portals

BBC - Utility Bill Pay (BBC-UBP)

BBC-UBP is our customer-facing IT portal. It allows 10,000 families to make electricity bill payments, track their usage, download bills, payment receipts, and more.

BBC Ops

BBC Ops is our Back Office IT Portal used by our 20 Operations employees. It helps manage customer data, generate invoices, track payment transactions, and streamline our operations.
